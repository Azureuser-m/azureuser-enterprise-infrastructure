Initial pfBlockerNG testing was affected by stale DNS cache. ChatGPT had been accessed before the UT1_Ai feed was enabled and remained reachable, while Claude AI, which had not been previously accessed, was blocked as expected. Clearing browser cache and flushing DNS did not change the result.
